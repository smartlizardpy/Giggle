About Giggle 🤪

Giggle is a chaotic, slightly cursed, but oddly functional search engine designed to make you laugh, cry, and maybe find what you were looking for.

Born from a pile of memes, duct tape, and a Raspberry Pi with self-esteem issues, Giggle delivers answers, sarcasm, and sponsored Rickrolls — all wrapped in a retro web vibe.

Why choose Giggle?

  • ChatGPT-style answers with questionable intelligence
  • British voice-powered text-to-speech 🎙️
  • Linux Mode for the real ones 🐧
  • Easter eggs that insult you lovingly
  • Fake ads so realistic you’ll almost click them

This site is powered by a talking toaster, three squirrels on caffeine, and a semi-reliable backend.
